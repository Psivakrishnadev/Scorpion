FindNews¶

Module contains methods to collect and parse news from the internet on various companies based on the provided database.

FindNews.getnews()[source]¶

Outer shell method to get news of companies present in the database. :return None:

FindNews.getnewsarticles(linklist, cName)[source]¶

Method takes list of news links and the name of company. Analyses the news on each page and collect the sentiment of the entire news. Stores the average sentiment of the news available for each company.

Parameters:
  • linklist (list) – List of all the hyperlinks
  • cName (str) – Company name
Return None:
FindNews.getnewslinks(Name)[source]¶

Methods takes string as an input which is the name of a particular company in the indian stock market. Does a google news search of the comapny name and returns the list of all the news links that belong to the news about that comapany.
